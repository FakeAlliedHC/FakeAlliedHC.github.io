OCS HC Awards

Ribbon Medal or Badge Discription

Allied Training Command Service Order

Awarded to those veteran Allied players who dedicate their efforts to assist and train our Allied forces. The Allied High Command gratefully acknowledges their achievements and dedication towards ensuring that our Allied forces are the best trained on the battlefield.

OCS Instructor Ribbon

Awarded to those Allied Officers who train and mentor new Allied High Command officers in the skills necessary to fulfill the responsibilities of their new positions. The Allied High Command gratefully acknowledges their achievements and dedication towards building a strong, qualified, cadre of AHC officers.

OCS Graduate Ribbon

Awarded to Allied personnel that have successfully completed Officers Candidate School.

OCS Honors Graduate Ribbon

Awarded to Allied personnel that have successfully completed Officers Candidate School with honors.
