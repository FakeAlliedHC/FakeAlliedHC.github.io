British HC Awards

Awarded by British HC officers to soldiers of either army

Ribbon Medal or Badge Discription

Cross of Great Britain

Awarded by the Commander of the British Expeditionary Forces to Allied personnel for exemplary actions above and beyond the call of duty in service of King and Country. God save the King!

RAF Pilot Wings

Awarded to any RAF pilot who completes 5 missions with at least 1 air-to-air kill or bomb on target.

RAF Naval Aviator Wings

Awarded to any RAF fleet arm pilot who destroys 10 enemy naval vessels.

Distinguished Service Cross

Awarded by the Commander of the British Army to specific individuals who have achieved outstanding combat achievements while serving in an allied army. This is the second highest Army award for combat actions.

Distinguished Service Cross

Awarded by the Commander of the Royal Air Force to specific individuals who have achieved outstanding combat achievements while serving in an allied air force. This is the second highest Air Force award for combat actions.

Combat Infantry Ribbon

For Allied infantryman who demonstrate quick thinking and show no fear, running headlong into the enemy.

Armored Combat Ribbon

Awarded for commendable performance by an individual while engaged in armored combat against the enemy.

Air Combat Ribbon

Awarded for commendable performance by an individual while engaged in aerial combat against enemy forces.

Battle Efficiency Ribbon

Awarded for commendable performance by an individual while engaged in naval combat against enemy forces.

RDP Bombing Ribbon

Death from above, dark angels, dambusters... Call them what you will, all those bomber pilots make true the Bomber Command's motto: "Strike Hard, Strike Sure". The Efforts in RDP bombing of this pilot make a difference in the war outcome.

Naval Support Ribbon

Awarded to naval personnel who excel at using naval power to support our other Allied ground and air forces, whether it be by ferrying troops, providing naval shore bombardments, or using fairmiles and naval shore units to defend an allied position. Awarded by Sqdn CO and above.

Close Air Support Ribbon

This award recognizes the daring and dashing CAS pilots who frequently brave the Luftwaffe and blazing Axis air defences to provide much needed air power in support of ground units.

Fighter Escort Ribbon

Awarded to those dedicated Allied fighter pilots who protect our bombers from the Luftwaffe, making sure that they get through to the target and back home time after time.

Engineer Ribbon

A combat engineer, or sapper, is a soldier who performs a variety of construction and demolition tasks under combat conditions. This award is presented to those Allied combat engineers who brave enemy fire to repair defensive AI positions, blow bridges and destroy enemy forward bases.

Forward Observer Ribbon

"The Forward Observer decoration is awarded to any Allied Member who, through stealth and knowledge of the map, reports on Axis movement first-hand. Through their observations Field Commanders can utilize such enemy movements to the good of the Allies while saving vital supply resources."

Anti-Air Defence Ribbon

Awarded for commendable performance by an individual while engaged in anti-aircraft combat against enemy forces.

Defence Ribbon

For individuals showing sustained commitment to the critical task of defending Allied positions. Whether it is defending a city, a cp, a bunker or a FB, those who dedicate themselves to defending Allied positions against the enemy are vital to the Allied war effort.

Artillery Support Ribbon

Awarded for commendable performance by an individual while engaged in anti-tank combat against enemy forces.

Covert Operations Ribbon

Bearing the insignia of the famed Combined Operations Command, this award recognizes the skill and daring of organized special forces operators who covertly menace the enemy. Such action has a sudden impact on the battlefield and shocks enemy moral, paving the way for the Allies to advance.

Airborne Paratrooper Ribbon

Awarded to those Allied personnel who display outstanding combat achievements while participating in airborne operations against the enemy.

Individual Operations Command Ribbon

Awarded by British High Command to officers and players who display outstanding leadership and teamwork in planning and leading small scale special operations. Awarded by Div CO and above.

Pilot's Ace Mission Award

Awarded to fighter pilots who show exceptional skill in the cockpit, scoring 5 confirmed Air to Air Victories in a single sortie while flying a RAF aircraft, as verified by awarding officer. Awarded by Air Brigade Commanders.

Elite Allied Fighter Ace Award

Awarded to any Allied pilot who scores 10 air-to-air victories with an RTB in a single sortie while flying a RAF aircraft.

Tanker Ace Ribbon

Awarded to any player who scores 5 tank kills with mechanized units with an RTB in a single sortie in a British or American Tank.

Mentioned in Dispatches With Bronze Palm

Awarded to those members whose commendable service merits special mention in After Action Reports or other official Allied communications.

Good Conduct Medal

This award is given by BDE officers to those individuals who show an exemplary sense of responsibility and manners on and off the battlefield, counseling where needed those players who would otherwise disrupt the good brotherhood feelings in the Allied side.

One Star Good Service Ribbon

For consistently carrying out requests during a session to a good level with a positive attitude that sets an example to others - a good team player.

Two Star Good Service Ribbon

For consistently carrying out requests over a period of time to a good level with a positive attitude that sets an example to others - a consistently good team player.

Three Star Good Service Ribbon

For consistently carrying out requests over a long period to a high level with a positive attitude that sets an example to others and encouraging others to do the same - a very good team player and a potential leader.

Individual Citation

This Citation is to be issued by Country Command or higher to Individuals who display outstanding Leadership and Teamwork in the field along side their fellow Allies.
