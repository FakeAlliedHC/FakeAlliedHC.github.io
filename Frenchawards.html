French HC Awards

Awarded by French HC officers to soldiers of either army

Ribbon Medal or Badge Discription

Croix de Lorraine

Awarded by the Commander of the Armee Francaise to Allied personnel for exemplary actions above and beyond the call of duty in the cause of the liberation of France. Vive la France!

FAF Pilot Wings

Awarded to any FAF pilot who completes 5 missions with at least 1 air-to-air kill or bomb on target.

FAF Naval Aviator Wings

Awarded to any FAF pilot who destroys 10 enemy naval vessels.

Médaille de l'Aéronautique

Awarded by the Commander of the Royal Air Force to specific individuals who have achieved outstanding combat achievements while serving in an allied air force. This is the second highest Air Force award for combat actions.

Croix de Poilu

For Allied infantryman who demonstrate quick thinking and show no fear, running headlong into the enemy.

Prix du pétrolier

Awarded for commendable performance by an individual while engaged in armored combat against the enemy.

Croix de l'arméede l'air

Awarded for commendable performance by an individual while engaged in aerial combat against enemy forces.

Médaille de la vaillance de la marine

Awarded for commendable performance by an individual while engaged in naval combat against enemy forces.

Ruban de bombardement RDP

Death from above, dark angels, dambusters... Call them what you will, all those bomber pilots make true the Bomber Command's motto: "Strike Hard, Strike Sure". The Efforts in RDP bombing of this pilot make a difference in the war outcome.

Médaille de combat de la marine

Awarded to naval personnel who excel at using naval power to support our other Allied ground and air forces, whether it be by ferrying troops, providing naval shore bombardments, or using fairmiles and naval shore units to defend an allied position. Must be awarded for actions taking place under enemy fire. Awarded by Sqdn CO and above.

Médaille d'excellence de la marine

Awarded to naval personnel who excel at using naval power to support our other Allied ground and air forces, whether it be by ferrying troops, providing naval shore bombardments, or using fairmiles and naval shore units to defend an allied position. Awarded by Sqdn CO and above.

Ruban d'ingénieur

A combat engineer, or sapper, is a soldier who performs a variety of construction and demolition tasks under combat conditions. This award is presented to those Allied combat engineers who brave enemy fire to repair defensive AI positions, blow bridges and destroy enemy forward bases.

Médaille de reconnaissance

the Médaille de reconnaissance is awarded to any Allied Member who, through stealth and knowledge of the map, reports on Axis movement first-hand. Through their observations Field Commanders can utilize such enemy movements to the good of the Allies while saving vital supply resources.

Ruban de chasseur de chars

For individuals showing sustained commitment to the critical task of defending Allied positions. Whether it is defending a city, a cp, a bunker or a FB, those who dedicate themselves to defending Allied positions against the enemy are vital to the Allied war effort.

Commandeur de Ordre des défenseurs

For individuals showing tremendous dedication and commitment to the critical task of defending Allied positions. Whether it is defending a city, a cp, a bunker or a FB, players awarded this medal have shown a monumental commitment to the defence of Allied postions. Awarded at the group level or higher, individual officers may only award this once per campaign .

Officier de Ordre des défenseurs

For individuals showing dedication and commitment to the critical task of defending Allied positions. Whether it is defending a city, a cp, a bunker or a FB, players awarded this medal have shown a strong commitment to the defence of Allied postions. Awarded at the brigade level or higher.

Chevaliar de Ordre des défenseurs

For individuals showing sustained commitment to the critical task of defending Allied positions. Whether it is defending a city, a cp, a bunker or a FB, those who dedicate themselves to defending Allied positions against the enemy are vital to the Allied war effort.

Commandeur de Ordre des As

Awarded to any Allied pilot who scores 15 air-to-air victories with an RTB in a single sortie while flying a RAF aircraft.

Officier de Ordre des As

Awarded to any Allied pilot who scores 10 air-to-air victories with an RTB in a single sortie while flying a RAF aircraft.

Chevalier de Ordre des As

Awarded to any Allied pilot who scores 5 air-to-air victories with an RTB in a single sortie while flying a RAF aircraft.

As de char

Awarded to any player who scores 5 tank kills with mechanized units with an RTB in a single sortie in a French or American Tank.

Médaille de la dévotion bronze

For consistently carrying out requests during a session to a good level with a positive attitude that sets an example to others - a good team player.

Médaille de la dévotion vermiel

For consistently carrying out requests over a period of time to a good level with a positive attitude that sets an example to others - a consistently good team player.

Médaille de la dévotion or

For consistently carrying out requests over a long period to a high level with a positive attitude that sets an example to others and encouraging others to do the same - a very good team player and a potential leader.
