French HC Awards

Ribbon Medal or Badge Discription

Croix de Lorraine

Awarded by the Commander of the Armee Francaise to Allied personnel for exemplary actions above and beyond the call of duty in the cause of the liberation of France. Vive la France!

FAF Pilot Wings

Awarded to any FAF pilot who completes 5 missions with at least 1 air-to-air kill or bomb on target.

FAF Naval Aviator Wings

Awarded to any FAF pilot who destroys 10 enemy naval vessels.

Médaille de reconnaissance

the Médaille de reconnaissance is awarded to any Allied Member who, through stealth and knowledge of the map, reports on Axis movement first-hand. Through their observations Field Commanders can utilize such enemy movements to the good of the Allies while saving vital supply resources.

As de char

Awarded to any player who scores 5 tank kills with mechanized units with an RTB in a single sortie in a French or American Tank.

Médaille de la dévotion bronze

For consistently carrying out requests during a session to a good level with a positive attitude that sets an example to others - a good team player.

Médaille de la dévotion vermiel

For consistently carrying out requests over a period of time to a good level with a positive attitude that sets an example to others - a consistently good team player.

Médaille de la dévotion or

For consistently carrying out requests over a long period to a high level with a positive attitude that sets an example to others and encouraging others to do the same - a very good team player and a potential leader.
